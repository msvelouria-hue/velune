Privacy Policy for Velune

Effective Date: April 19, 2026

Vuxo I Corp ("we," "our," or "us") operates the mobile application Velune (Bundle ID: co.velouria.vestiary). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Account information (email, display name, profile photo)
  • Profile information and preferences, such as gender, styling goals, hair profile, makeup preference level, and optional allergy or product-avoidance notes
  • Photos of clothing items
  • Approximate location for weather-based recommendations
  • Read-only calendar event data
  • Usage, activity, and preference data, such as wardrobe item counts, outfit requests, likes, dislikes, comments, notes, and interaction history used to improve recommendations

How We Use Information

To operate the app, manage accounts, generate outfit, hair, and makeup suggestions, personalize recommendations based on your profile and activity, improve features, and provide support.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google)
  • OpenAI API
  • OpenWeatherMap

Data Storage and Security

Data is stored on Google Cloud infrastructure and encrypted in transit.

Your Rights

You may access, export, or delete your data at any time.

Children's Privacy

Velune is intended for users aged 13 and older.
